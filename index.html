About Me

Moshood is a devout ahmadi muslim and a staunch Chelsea FC fan and he is fueled by his passion and ever ready attitude towards becoming an expert in varying fields of front-end and back-end technology. Moshood believes patience and persistence throughout a challenge is key to success - a tenet he lives out through his interests in web technology, freelancing, community development and sports.
