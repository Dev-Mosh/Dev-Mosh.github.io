Ahmadi Muslim Youth Convention - A spiritual & Physical Uplifment For All

"Every Ahmadi Muslim must prove to the doubters and sceptics that God does exist and that He is a Living God and that islam is the final religion revealed by Him" - Mirza Masroor Ahmad (A.T.B.A) message to Ahmadi youths.

The program which is themed "OBEDIENCE TO KHILAFAT" is meant to remind the youths the importance adhering to the rule of khilafat. The Majlis Khuddam-ul-Ahmmadiya, Nigeria (MKAN) 47th Annual National Youth Convention (IJTEMA) which was scheduled to be held at Offa, Kwara State, Nigeria has now been postponed due to the outbreak of corona virus which has indeed proved to be a pandemic. A key aspect of Ahmadi Muslim Youths responsibilities is to ensure effective and efficient youth's moral and spiritual development in this depraved society. A number of professionals, religious leaders, clergymen, government functionaries, youth leaders and several dignitaries will grace the convention. Among programmes lined up for the 3-days programme include Welfare Visitation of Prison Inmates, Free Health Care Delivery, Symposium, Voluntary Blood Donation, Educational Competitions, Sporting activities and so much more. Who is Avi Schiffman? The Brains Behind COVID-19 Tracking Website

As the world has been in total panic due to the pandemic which has claimed lives in multitude globally, a 17-Year Old from Washington State, U.S.A has developed a tracking website for COVID-19.

He launched this home made website to track the movement of coronavirus. Using a coding tactic known as "web-scraping", Avi's site collates data from different sources around the globe ranging from W.H.O, the C.D.D, Yonhap News Agency - South Korea and display the recent numbers of COVID-19 cases around the world. Visit nCoV2019.live to keep up with recent updates.
