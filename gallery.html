About Me

Moshood Abiola Badmus is a startup web developer and a graduate of computer science from the prestigious Federal Polytechnic Ilaro. Moshood is a devout ahmadi muslim and a staunch Chelsea FC fan and he is fueled by his passion and ever-ready attitude towards becoming an expert in varying fields of front-end and back-end technology.

He prides himself in working towards acting as a one-stop shop for web solutions ranging from website design and optimization, search engine optimization, digital and email marketing while also providing web tech trainings for tech enthusiasts. He considers himself a 'forever student', eager to both build on his academic foundations in computer science and stay in tune with the latest web technology trends through continued research and coursework. Moshood believes patience and persistence throughout a challenge is key to success - a tenet he lives out through his interests in web technology, freelancing, community development and sports.
